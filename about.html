About Us

Ekona Youths Sport Academy

We are dedicated to fostering the growth and development of young soccer players, ranging from under 10 to under 20, regardless of gender.

Our mission is to unearth and nurture the talents of teenagers in the beautiful game of soccer.

We firmly believe that the youth are the leaders of tomorrow, and we are committed to empowering them through the beautiful game. Our dedicated team of coaches and staff work tirelessly to create a supportive and challenging environment where players can thrive both on and off the field.
